What is Omakase?

Unlike any of the offerings in our main menu, omakase is a traditional Inazuman dining style in which the chef provides a meal tailored to the diner's preferences based on the diner's availability, budget, taste, and seasonality. Similar to a tasting menu, Uyuu has served everyone from the Raiden Shogun and elite members of the Tenryou Commission with their massive dining budget, to other diners looking for a seasonal, but affordable specialized meal.
Omakase usually suggests that nigirisushi is one of, if not the only, components of the menu, and while our customers do usually get plenty of individual nigiri plates, we've also served artfully presented salads to highlight an astrologist's emphasis of enjoying a simple dish that fills them up while working on a column. Omakase can even boil down to delivering the best kinds of tea that Narukami Island can offer, as per one request that a certain consultant specified during their meal at Uyuu Restaraunt.
Expect that our omakase offerings mostly involve nigirisushi, makisushi, and other traditional sushi rolls, but know that we can also offer other seafood, meat, or vegetable dishes we are confident to put our branding into. That said, we sadly do not offer vegan omakase menus at this time. However, vegetarian omakase menus can be requested in your online reservation.

To make an omakase reservation, fill out our Google Form specifically for omakase. Refer to our timesheet of available times to choose a time that works for us and you.
