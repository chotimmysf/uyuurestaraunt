    A Quick Overview

    Ever since the Raiden Shogun lifted the Sakoku Decree 30 years ago, we are proud to say that Inazuma has gone through plenty of technological changes that has made the country on par with, if not higher tech than, the Hydro nation of Fontaine. That being said, all citizens of Inazuma still fondly remember the luxuries of Inazuma's simple yet refined cuisine, and as a restaraunt located in the bustling heart of the nation, we find it critical to provide all wandering adventurers and travelers all across Teyvat to experience the wonders of fine Inazuman cuisine. Our head chef Okazaki Rikuto would like to welcome you all to experience Inazuma's wonderful cuisine here at Uyuu Restaraunt! We currently serve lunch and dinner, and as we are a busy restaraunt regularly hosting celebrities all over Teyvat, we ask that you reserve a time to sit with Chef Rikuto if we have open times on the days you wish to come.
