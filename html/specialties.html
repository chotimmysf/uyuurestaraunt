    All the special dishes we offer, and how to order them!

    Special Appetizers

    Quiet Elegance

    Available on the Narukami Island Set.

    Ebi Tempura with Special Decorations from Kamisato Ayato, Head of the Kamisato Estate and Yashiro Commission

    The arrangement is exquisite and elegant, much like the manner in which Ayato handles all his affairs. Any more, and it would have been excessive. Any less, and it would have proved a sight squandered. As you reach your chopsticks for that crystalline camellia, Ayato stops you... "That was something I made during sword practice. It's just a decoration."

    Warmth

    Available on the Main Menu and the Narukami Island Set.

    Miso Soup with Special Touches from Thoma, Housekeeper of the Kamisato Clan

    The intricate appearance of this dish belies the simple ingredients used. But take a sip, and Thoma's sunshine smile comes straight to mind. Perhaps this is his special ability: warming the hearts of others using the simplest of ingredients.

    Stunning Stratagems

    The modified version with tofu instead of egg is available on the Main Menu as "Sangonomiya Fish Tail Inari". The original egg version is available on the Watatsumi Island Set Menu. You may substitute for the egg version of this dish outside of the set menu at no extra charge. Just specify the change on the notes of your online order or tell the server when they take your table's order.

    Inari presented like Chakinzushi. Instead of using egg sheets, we use fried tofu to encase the sushi. Decorations are inspired by
    Sangonomiya Kokomi, the Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island

    The tofu is wrapped up almost like a package, and the fish tail at the end is both small and very cute. It really makes you wonder what's inside the package, but at the same time, you can't bear to eat such a beautiful-looking thing... You glance at the crystal-clear smile in Kokomi's eyes. Ah, darn it, you've fallen for her trick, haven't you?

    Special Entrees

    All-Weather Beauty

    Available on the Main Menu.

    Ordinary mackarel with flavors of the definition of a free, all-embracing nature.
    From Kaedehara Kazuha and the dried fish on Liyue's Crux Fleet.

    Made from fish that have been dried on the ship. Kazuha claims that they were completely ordinary fish.
    But this flavor... this must surely be the definition of free, all-embracing nature, no?

    Fukuuchi Udon

    Available on the Main Menu.

    Udon made by the Lady Kitsune Guuji herself.

    The fried tofu in here is specially made by the solemn Lady Kitsune. It is said that if you eat it all at one go with sincerity in your heart, you will have the protection of the Great Kitsune -- misfortune shall flee and fortune abound!

    Victorious Legend

    Available on the Watatsumi Set Menu.

    Ramen from the General of Her Excellency. Also sometimes recommended by Miss Hina.

    The side dishes have been piled high to form a sight as moving as Gorou's own motivations. As you finish the noodles and soup all in one go and pat your stomach in satisfication, you look up and see Gorou... smiling radiantly as his tail wags from side to side (the latter probably unbeknownst to him!)

    Special Desserts

    Rainbow Aster

    Available on the Main Menu.

    A special Yakult, Sprite, and Ube Creme drink featured in the famous light novel "Pretty Please, Kitsune Guuji?"

    Having featured in the famous light novel "Pretty Please, Kitsune Guuji?", it has since been brought to life by Sailing Breeze in collaboration with Yae Publishing House, along with its fascinating flavour. As this version does not contain any bean products, it is slightly different from the special limited edition that was available during the Irodori Festival, but this also means that there is no risk of triggering any bean allergies.

    Snow on the Hearth

    Available on the Narukami Set Menu.

    Sakuramochi with a special seal from Kamisato Ayaka of the Yashiro Commission.

    This is almost like a small, lovely, and intricate artwork, with its pale red outer coat covering the feelings that the young maiden began to harbor from that moment on. If one had the honor to enjoy such a treat while having tea with a princess as graceful and noble as a white heron, one would surely yearn to have that moment stretch into eternity.

    Summer Festival Fish

    Available on the Narukami Set Menu.

    Tricolor Dango with the shape of Summer Fish and a glistening amber glaze coating the fish, sponsored by the Owner of Naganohara Fireworks.

    Watching the fireworks, your hands full of the snacks Yoimiya's given you, her carefree, child-like laughter in your ear as the pyrotechnic burst overhead... Amid this unique symphony of sounds, the plump goldfish in your hand almost looks like it might start swimming at any moment...
