    Operations

    When is Uyuu Restaraunt open?

    Uyuu Restaraunt is open everyday, from 6:00am - midnight.

    What is your policy on parties of 6 or more?

    Uyuu Restaraunt will charge an automatic 12% gratiuty fee to all tables of 6 or more. Parties of 6 and more are highly suggested to reserve a time through our Reservations page or through our Yelp page.

    Are there any private dining options?

    Uyuu Restaraunt does have a few private rooms that can host up to a maximum of 12 people each. For these reservations, please note that you would like a private room and we will accept by a case-by-case condition.

    How is an Omakase reservation different from the typical reservation?

    Omakase is specifically tailored for only 10 people at a time, and cannot be reserved through a typical reservation. In order to enjoy our omakase, you will need to reserve a time slot specifically on our Omakase page as we use a separate dining spot from the main menu for our omakase offerings.
